Predictive Analytics: Real Estate & Macro-Economics

Using Python, Random Forests, and Gradient Boosting to Forecast Market Volatility.

Exploratory Data Analysis

Correlation between Fed Funds Rate and Median Housing Price (2014-2026).

Model Performance

Comparison of R² scores across training and testing datasets.

Business Questions & Methodology

  • Primary Objective: Identify if interest rate lags can reliably predict a 5% shift in housing prices within 6 months.
  • Data Source: Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) - MSPUS, FEDFUNDS.
  • Preprocessing: Min-Max Scaling and 3-month lagging on feature sets.

Ethical Interpretability

Predictive modeling in real estate must account for algorithmic bias. Models that rely heavily on historical zip code data can perpetuate "digital redlining," unfairly influencing lending rates or investment in marginalized communities. This project implements SHAP values to ensure feature transparency.
