Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of Federal Interest Rates and Unemployment Rates on the U.S. Median Home Sales Price using data sourced from the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED).

Business Question: To what extent can macro-economic shifts predict housing market volatility for institutional investors?

Exploratory Data Analysis

The correlation matrix revealed a strong inverse relationship between interest rates and median housing prices over a 10-year horizon.

Ethics & Interpretability

While predictive models offer high accuracy, they introduce risks in the housing market. Automated valuation models (AVMs) can inadvertently accelerate gentrification by targeting specific zip codes, potentially displacing low-income residents. Transparency in feature weighting is essential to ensure equitable housing access.
